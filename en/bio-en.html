About Me

Hi, I'm Dario, I'm 24 years old and I live between Segovia and Mallorca.

Even when I was very young, I was competitive—with my friends, my siblings, my enemies... but above all, with myself. I think that's why, after many ups and downs, I ordered a mathematics salad for dinner, dressed with strips of engineering and a vinaigrette of computer science. I suppose at the time it was a way of challenging myself. Math had always been hard to swallow, but when I ate it, I felt that sensation you get when you hurt yourself doing something you love. The fact is, I decided to keep eating, and finally—very finally—it seems I'm managing to finish a dish that, despite being a salad, is as heavy as a black pudding yogurt.

From the pulpit provided by the knowledge acquired during my university years, I can assure you that being demanding with oneself is the main ingredient to achieving one's goals. As the band Oques Grases sings:

"potser que ho donguis tot, i no ho aconsegueixis
et quedis sense forces
i tot i així segueixis."

For me, the path is clear: fall, get up, stumble, straighten up, cry, smile... and repeat until you make it. Saying it is easy; clearly, not everyone can do it. Depending on who judges, I may have succeeded or not. For me, I believe I've managed to hit the high note—perhaps with a few cracks in my voice—but in the end, I sang it, which is what counts.

I want this portfolio to serve as a thank you to everyone who has contributed something during my formative years, from Esteban in Cantimpalos, to Casacuberta in Barcelona. I think they are two clear examples of what teachers can achieve in young minds.


Thanks to everyone, this goes out to you.

Dario
